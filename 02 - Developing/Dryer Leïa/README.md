Dryer Model “Leïa”
==================

Specifications
--------------

Initial requirements

-   to dry 80kg of fresh herbs per week

-   drying time 2.5 days

-   40kg workload

-   thick layer loading (4 kg/m²)

Derived requirements

-   10m² drying surface

-   21 drawers of about 0,48m² (0,6\*0,8)
